Project Description

Inventory management system is an application or system that I built as a default feature of the Inventory module in ERP Hashmicro, Some of the features in this system include:

  • Receiving Notes as a menu for recording incoming goods
  • Delivery Order as a menu for recording outgoing goods
  • Internal Transfer as a menu for recording the movement of goods between warehouses without transit warehouses
  • Internal Transit Transfer as a menu for recording the movement of goods between warehouses and transit warehouses
  • Product Usage as a recording of assets that are damaged or can be recycled
  • Stock Move recording
  • Inventory adjustments and so on
